Rent Reviews for Tenants • Leeds

Are you a commercial tenant? We can help reduce your rent.

For over 11 years, T1 Asset Management has assisted tenants across various commercial properties — from retail outlets and restaurants to offices, surgeries, warehouses, and industrial facilities — when confronting rent reviews or lease renewals.

Leeds rent reviews

Can we reduce your rent?

If you're a tenant in Leeds or anywhere else in the UK facing a commercial rent review, the lease terms will significantly impact your rent. Talking to T1 Asset Management at an early date will ensure that you do not fall foul of time limits which could have serious repercussions on your tenancy.

As specialist commercial rent review surveyors we can help to ensure that an excessive demand is reduced and a fair open market rental value achieved.

Rent reviews are typically ‘upward only,’ so our goal is to keep the rent unchanged and, where possible, minimize any proposed increases. However, during lease renewals, T1 Asset Management can sometimes negotiate a reduction in rent compared to the previous amount.

For tenants of shops, restaurants, offices, or warehouse/industrial spaces facing rent reviews or lease renewals, our expert surveyors specialize in securing fair terms and ensuring the lowest possible rents for our clients.

Rent reviews can be a daunting challenge for tenants, but we handle the process efficiently on your behalf. Where necessary, we work on finding creative, cost-effective solutions.

We understand that tenants paying £20,000 per year for smaller properties or £500,000 annually for larger premises may be in a vulnerable position when faced with a landlord seeking a significant rent hike — or struggling to navigate the complexities of the 1954 Landlord & Tenant Act.
